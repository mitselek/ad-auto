# infinity-grind — rack up infinities for banked-infinity requirements.
# Stage: any time a Reality/achievement wants lots of (banked) infinities.
# Setup: save a passive/infinity-oriented tree as preset name GRIND.
#        Adjust the 1e6 target to taste (or use an Automator constant).
# Repeat toggle: OFF (one-shot; ends with an eternity to bank them).

studies nowait load name GRIND
auto eternity off
auto infinity 5s
notify "grinding infinities"
wait infinities >= 1e6
# TS191 banks a share of your infinities on eternity.
eternity nowait
notify "infinity grind done"
stop
